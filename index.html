Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

Overview

SpellShot ("we", "our", or "the app") is an AI-powered photo transformation app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

By using SpellShot, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

· · ·

Information We Collect

  • Anonymous User ID — We assign each user an anonymous identifier via Firebase Authentication. No name, email, or personal details are required.
  • Photos — When you use the transformation feature, your selected photo is temporarily sent to our AI processing service (Replicate) to generate the wizard image. We do not store your original photos on our servers.
  • Credit Balance — Your in-app credit balance is stored in Firebase Firestore, linked only to your anonymous ID.
  • Usage Data — Basic usage statistics such as number of transformations performed are stored anonymously.
  • Purchase Data — In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We only receive confirmation of a successful purchase to add credits to your account.
· · ·

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and return your AI wizard photo transformation
  • To manage your credit balance across sessions
  • To verify and fulfill in-app purchases
  • To improve app performance and user experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

· · ·

Third-Party Services

SpellShot uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — Anonymous authentication and data storage. Privacy Policy
  • Replicate — AI image processing. Your photo is sent to Replicate's servers for transformation and is not retained after processing. Privacy Policy
  • Apple In-App Purchase — Payment processing for credit purchases. Privacy Policy
· · ·

Data Retention

Your anonymous user data (credit balance, usage count) is retained as long as you use the app. If you uninstall the app and reinstall it, a new anonymous ID is created and previous data is no longer accessible.

Photos submitted for transformation are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers after the transformation is complete.

· · ·

Children's Privacy

SpellShot is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

· · ·

Your Rights

  • You may request deletion of your anonymous data by contacting us
  • You may opt out of the app at any time by uninstalling it
  • You may contact us with any privacy-related concerns
· · ·

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
